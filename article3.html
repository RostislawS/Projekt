SHANGHAI MAJOR 2024

Major tournament section

Players now have access to the Major tournament section, where they can buy tournament items, make predictions, and more. By purchasing the Perfect World Shanghai Major 2024 spectator pass, you will receive an upgradeable tournament coin. With the activated pass, you can upgrade your coin and earn additional souvenir sets by making predictions for the tournament.You'll also be added to a prediction leaderboard where you can compare your progress with your friends. All players with prime status can make predictions for free! However, you can complete challenges of a certain stage of the tournament and receive rewards for this only if you activate the pass before the start of this stage.
